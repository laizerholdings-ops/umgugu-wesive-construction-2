Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers.

What areas do you service?

We are based in Verulam, Kwa-Zulu Natal, and service the surrounding regions. For large-scale projects, we may consider locations further afield. Please contact us to discuss your project's specific location.

How do I get a quote for my project?

You can request a free, no-obligation quote by filling out the contact form on our website, calling us directly, or sending us a message on WhatsApp. We'll schedule a site visit if necessary to provide an accurate estimate.

What is the typical duration of a paving project?

The duration depends on the size and complexity of the project. A standard residential driveway can take a few days, while a large industrial parking lot may take a week or more. We provide a detailed project timeline with every quote.

Our Comprehensive Services

At UMGUGU WESIZWE CONSTRUCTION (PTY) LTD, we believe that a strong foundation is the key to any successful project. Road Construction

We provide end-to-end road construction services, handling everything from initial surveying and excavation to base preparation and final surfacing. Our focus is on creating durable, safe, and reliable road networks that serve communities and businesses for decades.

  • Site clearing, earthworks, and grading.
  • Stormwater drainage and culvert installation.
  • Sub-base and base course construction.

Our expert asphalt and tar surfacing services provide a sleek, professional finish for any area.   • Line marking and traffic control signage.
